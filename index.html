Strategic Implications

01

The Volume-to-Value Shift

While enrolling a peak cohort of N=1,200 is a major volume achievement, translating this into institutional value (our 75% mandate) demands ongoing strategic value activities—specifically, deploying highly scalable, prescriptive early alerts.

02

Cross-Functional Execution

Algorithms do not retain students; advisors and faculty do. Success dictates pushing IR's predictive risk scores directly into frontline CRM tools (e.g., Salesforce, Classter) for immediate case management.

03

Closing the Target Gap

Closing the 3.5% gap to our 2028 Strategic Goal is a mathematically exact science. It demands tracking absolute student volume against localized, high-impact interventions.
